City Latitude vs Wind Speed

In the latitude vs wind speed scatter plot shown below, we are able to observe two main trends. First, the northern hemisphere experiences higher windspeeds than the southern one. Second, slower wind speeds are more common than higher windspeeds. The highest windspeeds tend to be quite far north, at 80 degrees latitude, which is at the north pole.
